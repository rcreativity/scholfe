About Us

Scholfe was conceptualized by a team of ex-bankers who have several years of experience in the educational funding ecosystem, and have understood the problems of educational institutions and parents. The idea is to have an impact on both the provider and the receiver of education; an urge to EDUCATE and ELEVATE.
Launched in 2020, we aspire to be the preferred solution provider to K-12 and Upskill institutions through a completely digital platform. Providing seamless onboarding for institutions and assisting parents in spreading their educational costs into smaller instalments; while getting rewarded in the process.
We are an IMPACT-Tech company, using cutting-edge technology to bring financial planning solutions to the educational ecosystem.

FAQ

What does Scholfe do?
Scholfe provides loans to the deserving and needy candidates at ZERO PERCENT interest so that they can easily pursue their dream education without any interest burden.
What is the eligibility criteria to avail an education loan? Am I eligible for one?
Any parents/ guardians/individuals who are looking for financial support for child’s/one self’s education at any Institute either recognized or partnered with Scholfe is eligible to apply for a loan from Scholfe. A Co-Applicant is mandatory if the applicant is not working or has no prior work experience.
What type of courses does Scholfe provide loans for?
An Education loan from Scholfe will cover up to a 100% of only the Course fee depending upon the strength of the applicant’s profile and credit worthiness of the principal borrower.
How much time does it take to process an application?
Scholfe will convey its decision within 2 to 3 hours for applicants choosing to apply at any recognized or partnered Institute. If the candidate chooses to apply for Scholfe’s non- partnered institute then we will get back to him/her within 24 hours from the time of receipt of the application, provided the application is complete in all respects.
How and when will Scholfe disburse the loan?
The loan will be disbursed in full or in suitable instalments on the day that the application is complete or on the day of the commencement of the course taking into account the requirement of funds and/or fee schedule as assessed by Scholfe, directly to the Institute of the applicant’s choice upon completion of all the necessary formalities.
Do educational loans from Scholfe require any collateral or security?
Scholfe may or may not insist on a collateral or security in specific cases depending upon the quantum of loan amount, the strength of the applicant’s profile and the credit worthiness of the principal borrower.
What is the schedule of repayment for an educational loan availed from Scholfe?
The Repayment Schedule will commence immediately from the date of disbursal or Commencement of the Course at the Institute with the candidate paying the first instalment on that date itself.
Are there any additional fees or charges to be paid to Scholfe?
Currently, Scholfe will not charge any no sort of additional fees or charges of any nature.
What is the maximum tenure for an education loan availed from Scholfe?
The maximum tenure of a loan that can be availed from Scholfe is 36 months. However, Scholfe reserves the right to define the maximum tenure in every case as per credit-worthiness of the borrower.
What will be the role of my guardian in the entire process?
The parent(s) or guardian of the applicant would be treated as a Co-Applicant of this Education Loan if the applicant is not working. His or her role would be similar to that of a Primary Debtor.
