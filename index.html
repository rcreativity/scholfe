About Us

Scholfe was conceptualized by a team of ex-bankers who have several years of experience in the educational funding ecosystem, and have understood the problems of educational institutions and parents. The idea is to have an impact on both the provider and the receiver of education; an urge to EDUCATE and ELEVATE.
Launched in 2020, we aspire to be the preferred solution provider to K-12 and Upskill institutions through a completely digital platform. Providing seamless onboarding for institutions and assisting parents in spreading their educational costs into smaller instalments; while getting rewarded in the process.
We are an IMPACT-Tech company, using cutting-edge technology to bring financial planning solutions to the educational ecosystem.
